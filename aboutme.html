About Me

Hi! I'm Ayla I'm a graphic design and UX/UI student with a deep passion for visual communication, accessibility, and storytelling. This past winter and spring, I had the incredible opportunity to study abroad in London, a city that challenged the way I navigate the world, both physically and creatively.
