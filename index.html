During Winter 2024 for my international experience credit for Stamps School of Art & Design at UMich, I studied in the graphic design programme at UAL Camberwell College of Arts in London, where I was surrounded by both local and international students. When I discovered cheap flights from London and with my flexible schedule, I simply couldn't pass up the chance to explore more. After visiting different cities, I fell even more in love with traveling and experiencing new cultures.
